This is an article I wrote for the Odyssey. It's about places where students can use their student I.D. and get some pretty great discounts. Preparing For Careers in Digital Advertising and Social Media

This was a story I wrote for Mass Comm Week at Texas State. A panel of alumni came to campus to give advice to students on applying for jobs after college and how to prepare themselves for those careers. I also live-tweeted this event, which was something I had done once before. It was cool to practice that again and use social media as a part of journalism.

The Impact of Scholarships

This was a senior journalism project about the impact of scholarships on students at Texas State University.  Over the course of the spring semester I interviewed and filmed students at the Texas State campus to learn more of just how scholarships can impact a college student. This project really taught me a lot  about interviewing and gathering information on deadlines. There is so much that I took away from this and I know that it will benefit me tremendously in the future.
